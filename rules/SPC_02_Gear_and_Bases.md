# Super Gear and Bases

## Super Strength & Encumbrance

For characters with Strength above d12, use the table below. This replaces the standard encumbrance rules to reflect comic book power levels.

- **Encumbered:** Exceeding the Encumbrance Level (EL) inflicts a -2 penalty to Pace (min 1"), running rolls, Agility, all Agility-linked skills, and Vigor rolls vs. Fatigue.
    
- **Maximum Weight:** A character can lift/carry a maximum of **four times** the EL.
    
- **Vigor Limit:** At three times the EL, a hero can move at Pace 1 for rounds equal to Vigor; thereafter, succeed at a Vigor roll every round or take Fatigue.

### Capes

Decorative/Short capes have no effect. Long capes ($50, Min Str d4) add +1 to Intimidation or Persuasion. Grappling/Physical tests vs. the wearer are at +2.

- **Armored:** +2* ballistic armor (doesn't stack). Can be used as a Medium Shield (+2 Parry, -2 Cover) if one hand is free. ($500, +4 lbs).
    
- **Fireproof:** Reduces fire damage by 4. ($300, +2 lbs).
    
- **Gliding:** Pace 6, drop 10' vertical per 100' horizontal. ($500, +4 lbs).
    
- **Obscuring:** Direct attacks against wearer are at -1 (unless Bound/Entangled). ($500, +2 lbs).
    

## Weapons

### Close Combat & Special Weapons

- **Nullifier Rod/Gun:** Target makes Vigor roll (-2 with raise) or loses super powers. Recovers 1/round (2 with raise) on free Vigor roll.
    
- **Gauntlets:** Str+d4, Min Str d6. +4 Armor to hands. Prevents fine manipulation.
    
- **Pulse Weapons:** Can expend 2 ammo for double damage; Critical Failure explodes for 3d6 damage to user.
    
- **Foam Thrower:** Small Blast Template. Evasion roll (-2 with raise) or become Bound. Hardness 10.
    

### Weapon Modifications

- **Chain/Vibro:** +d6 damage, AP +2. Very loud. ($500, +2 lbs).
    
- **Energy Weapon:** Damage +1 die type, AP +4. Heavy Weapon. Increases Min Str. ($750, +1 lb).
    
- **Molecular Blade:** +2 damage, AP = half weapon damage die (e.g., +4 AP for d8). ($500, +1 lb).
    
- **Power Weapon:** +d6 damage (+d10 with raise). ($500, +1 lb).
    

## Bases (Headquarters)

Bases are constructed through narrative Acquisition and the following steps:

### Step 1: Advantage (1d6)

1. **Exotic Location:** Space station, volcano, etc. (Can be Hidden).
    
2. **Hallowed:** Start session with +1 Benny. Citizens are Friendly.
    
3. **Inspiring:** Team members increase Spirit one die type.
    
4. **Mentor:** Random player each session gets Conviction (anecdote required).
    
5. **Powerful:** +5 SPP (Power Levels I-III) or +10 SPP (IV-V). Must take shared Major Hindrance.
    
6. **Renowned:** All members gain +1 permanent die type in a chosen Trait.
    

### Step 2: Complication (1d6)

1. **Contested:** Team gains Enemy (Minor).
    
2. **Crumbling:** High chance of Collapse encounter.
    
3. **Foreboding:** Team members have Bad Luck Hindrance.
    
4. **Very Public:** Daily crowds, nosy reporters, collateral damage risk.
    
5. **Intruders:** Frequently invaded by creatures/demons/AI.
    
6. **Remote:** Distance requires special travel equipment/upgrades.
    

### Step 4: Upgrades

When the group earns an Advance, they can add one Room Upgrade.

- **Advanced Firewalls:** -8 to foe's Hacking rolls.
    
- **Medical Center:** +2 to Healing rolls.
    
- **Science Lab / Occult Library:** +2 to Science/Research or Occult rolls.
    
- **Training Room:** All members gain +1 Toughness.
    
- **Teleporter:** Minute recharge. Failure = 2d10 miles off; Critical Failure = off-world.
