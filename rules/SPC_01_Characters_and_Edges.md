# Super Powers: Characters and Edges

Super-powered characters follow the standard SWADE creation rules but receive the **Super Powers Edge** for free, granting them a pool of Super Power Points (SPP).

## Campaign Power Levels

The Game Master chooses a Power Level for the campaign, which dictates starting Super Power Points and the maximum points allowed in a single power.

|   |   |   |   |
|---|---|---|---|
|**Power Level**|**SPP**|**Power Limit**|**Example Genre**|
|**I**|15|5|Early pulp heroes, street-level teens.|
|**II**|30|10|Street fighters, veteran pulp heroes.|
|**III**|45|15|"Four Color" heroes (Standard Supers).|
|**IV**|60|20|Heavy Hitters (Global defenders).|
|**V**|75|25|Galactic Guardians (Cosmic level).|

### Power Limits

Characters cannot spend more than the campaign's **Power Limit** on any single power (after all modifiers are applied). This ensures the "feel" of the setting is maintained.

## New Skill: Focus (Spirit)

Focus reflects a hero's ability to use abilities not covered by other Traits. It is used as the default activation skill for many super powers. It is also the arcane skill for the Arcane Background (Gifted).

## New Hindrances

- **Alien Form (Major):** Completely alien appearance. Healing rolls are at -4. Social challenges in humanoid society.
    
- **Dependency (Major):** Must consume or have contact with a substance for 1 hour every 24 hours or lose powers/take Fatigue.
    
- **Dependent (Minor/Major):** A Novice Wild Card without powers the hero must protect.
    
- **Distinctive Appearance (Minor):** Unique look (blue skin, etc.) present even out of costume.
    
- **Environmental Weakness (Minor):** Susceptible to a Power Type (e.g., Fire). Takes +4 damage and -4 to resistance rolls.
    
- **Grim (Minor):** Humorless. Automatically Provoked on a successful Taunt.
    
- **Idealistic (Minor):** Believes in absolute truth and justice; struggles with "gray" moral areas.
    
- **Megalomaniac (Major):** Must lead. No benefits from Command effects; -4 penalty to Support others.
    
- **Monologuer (Major):** When Action Card is a Club, must make Smarts -2 or spend turn gloating/lecturing.
    
- **Power Negation (Minor/Major):** Common or rare substance/condition robs hero of ALL super powers when within 6".
    
- **Reckless (Major):** Critical Failures on activation rolls cause powers to go wildly out of control.
    
- **Secret Identity (Major):** Enemies will target the hero's civilian life if discovered.
    
- **Terminally Ill (Minor/Major):** Suffer -1 or -2 to resist Fatigue. Critical Failure on Vigor roll leads to death this session.
    
- **Transformation (Minor/Major):** Must trigger a change to use powers. Action/Focus roll (Minor) or longer/external trigger (Major).
    
- **Vulnerability (Minor/Major):** Exposure to a substance causes the Distracted state (Minor) plus Fatigue rolls (Major).
    

## New Edges

### Background Edges

#### Super Powers

- **Requirements:** Novice
    
- **Effect:** Grants Super Power Points (SPP) based on the Campaign Power Level. Free in most supers settings. These points are separate from Arcane Background Power Points.
    

#### The Best There Is

- **Requirements:** Novice
    
- **Effect:** The hero has one exceptionally potent ability. Their Power Limit for a single power is increased to **half** their maximum SPP (round down) instead of one-third.
    

### Combat Edges

#### Take the Hit

- **Requirements:** Seasoned, Iron Jaw, Vigor d10+
    
- **Effect:** Hero is exceptionally tough. Gain a free reroll on Soak rolls or Vigor rolls to resist Knockout Blows.
    

### Leadership Edges

#### Team Leader

- **Requirements:** Seasoned, Command, Common Bond, Natural Leader
    
- **Effect:** Allies within Command Range may exchange Bennies as if they all had the Common Bond Edge.
    

### Social Edges

#### Dynamic Duo

- **Requirements:** Novice, Spirit d8
    
- **Effect:** Choose one specific partner. When assisting them in a Combined Attack, add both the Support skill bonus to hit and the extra damage for Combined Attacks.
    

#### Sidekick

- **Requirements:** Wild Card, Veteran
    
- **Effect:** As per the SWADE Edge, but the sidekick has SPP equal to the mentor's **Power Limit** (though the sidekick themselves has no Power Limit).
