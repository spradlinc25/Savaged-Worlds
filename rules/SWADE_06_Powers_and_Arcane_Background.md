# Powers and Arcane Backgrounds

This section covers the mechanics of using "magic," whether it be arcane spells, divine miracles, psychic abilities, or weird science gadgets.

## Arcane Backgrounds (AB)

To use powers, a character must take the **Arcane Background** Edge. This determines their starting Power Points (PP), starting powers, arcane skill, and the penalty for a Critical Failure (rolling a 1 on both the skill die and the Wild Die).

### 1. Magic

- **Arcane Skill:** Spellcasting (Smarts)
    
- **Starting Power Points:** 10
    
- **Starting Powers:** 3
    
- **Backlash:** A Critical Failure causes the caster to take a level of Fatigue.
    

### 2. Miracles

- **Arcane Skill:** Faith (Spirit)
    
- **Starting Power Points:** 10
    
- **Starting Powers:** 3
    
- **Backlash:** A Critical Failure causes the caster to take a level of Fatigue.
    

### 3. Psionics

- **Arcane Skill:** Psionics (Smarts)
    
- **Starting Power Points:** 10
    
- **Starting Powers:** 3
    
- **Backlash:** A Critical Failure causes the caster to take a level of Fatigue.
    

### 4. Weird Science

- **Arcane Skill:** Weird Science (Smarts)
    
- **Starting Power Points:** 15
    
- **Starting Powers:** 2
    
- **Backlash:** A Critical Failure causes the device to malfunction. It cannot be used again until repaired (Requires 1d4 hours and a Repair roll).
    
- **Special:** Each power is a separate physical device. The inventor can "share" their devices with allies.
    

### 5. Gifted

- **Arcane Skill:** Focus (Spirit)
    
- **Starting Power Points:** 15
    
- **Starting Powers:** 1
    
- **Backlash:** A Critical Failure causes the caster to take a level of Fatigue.
    
- **Special:** Gifted characters manifest innate abilities (like a superhero's laser eyes or flight). They do not use Trappings; their powers are fixed in their manifestation.
    

## Casting Mechanics

- **The Roll:** The caster rolls their arcane skill. The standard Target Number is 4.
    
- **Power Points (PP):** Powers cost PP to activate. PP are deducted _whether the roll succeeds or fails_.
    
- **Success:** The power activates as described.
    
- **Raise:** The power activates and usually gains a bonus effect (e.g., more damage, longer duration).
    
- **Failure:** The power fails, and the PP are lost.
    
- **Shorting:** A caster can choose to cast a power with fewer PP than required. They suffer a penalty to their casting roll equal to the number of missing PP. (e.g., Casting a 3 PP power with only 1 PP inflicts a -2 penalty to the arcane skill roll). If the roll fails while Shorting, the caster automatically takes a level of Fatigue.
    

## Recovering Power Points

- **Base Rate:** Casters recover 5 PP per hour of rest.
    
- **Rapid Recharge Edge:** Recovers 10 PP per hour.
    
- **Improved Rapid Recharge Edge:** Recovers 20 PP per hour.
    

## Trappings

Trappings are the narrative "flavor" of a power (e.g., fire, ice, holy light, acid). Trappings can alter the mechanics of a power in minor ways, usually subject to GM approval.

- **Elemental Effects:** A fire _Bolt_ might ignite flammable materials. An ice _Bolt_ might extinguish a small fire.
    
- **Synergy:** If a target has an Environmental Weakness to a specific Trapping (e.g., a Fire Elemental vs. a water Trapping), the power inflicts +4 damage and the target subtracts 4 from rolls to resist its effects.
    

## Power Modifiers

Power Modifiers allow a caster to customize a power on the fly by spending extra Power Points. These stack with the base cost.

- **Armor Piercing (+1 to +3 PP):** The attack ignores 2 points of Armor for each PP spent, up to a maximum of AP 6 (3 PP).
    
- **Fatigue (+2 PP):** If the power successfully hits or affects the target, it also inflicts a level of Fatigue.
    
- **Glow/Shroud (+1 PP):** The power causes the target to emit light (canceling illumination penalties) OR shroud them in darkness (-1 to be hit).
    
- **Heavy Weapon (+2 PP):** The attack counts as a Heavy Weapon.
    
- **Hinder/Hurry (+1 PP):** Reduces the target's Pace by 2 (Hinder) OR increases their Pace by 2 (Hurry) for the duration of the power.
    
- **Lingering Damage (+2 PP):** The target takes the power's base damage again on their next turn.
    

## Core Power List (Extract)

_Note: This is a mechanical summary of the most common core powers used for calculations._

### Armor

- **Cost:** 2
    
- **Range:** Smarts
    
- **Duration:** 5
    
- **Effect:** Grants +2 Armor to the target. A Raise grants +4 Armor.
    

### Barrier

- **Cost:** 2
    
- **Range:** Smarts
    
- **Duration:** 5
    
- **Effect:** Creates a straight wall 5" (10 yards) long and 1" (2 yards) thick. The wall has Hardness 10. A Raise increases Hardness to 12.
    

### Blast

- **Cost:** 3
    
- **Range:** Smarts x2
    
- **Duration:** Instant
    
- **Effect:** Creates a Medium Blast Template. Characters inside suffer 2d6 damage. A Raise increases damage to 3d6.
    

### Bolt

- **Cost:** 1
    
- **Range:** Smarts x2
    
- **Duration:** Instant
    
- **Effect:** Fires a magical projectile for 2d6 damage. A Raise increases damage to 3d6.
    
- **Modifier - Damage (+2 PP):** The _Bolt_ deals 3d6 damage (4d6 with a Raise).
    

### Boost/Lower Trait

- **Cost:** 2
    
- **Range:** Smarts
    
- **Duration:** 5 (Boost) / Instant (Lower)
    
- **Effect:** * _Boost:_ Increases a chosen Attribute or Skill by one die type. A Raise increases it by two die types.
    
    - _Lower:_ Opposed by the target's Spirit. Reduces a chosen Attribute or Skill by one die type. A Raise reduces it by two die types.
        

### Deflection

- **Cost:** 3
    
- **Range:** Smarts
    
- **Duration:** 5
    
- **Effect:** Attacks against the target suffer a -2 penalty. A Raise increases the penalty to -4.
    

### Healing

- **Cost:** 3
    
- **Range:** Touch
    
- **Duration:** Instant
    
- **Effect:** Removes one Wound from the target (two Wounds with a Raise). Must be cast within the "Golden Hour" (one hour of taking the damage). Suffer the target's Wound penalties to the casting roll.
    

### Protection

- **Cost:** 1
    
- **Range:** Smarts
    
- **Duration:** 5
    
- **Effect:** Reduces damage taken from all sources by 2. A Raise reduces damage by 4. (Note: This is separate from _Armor_, as it reduces damage directly rather than increasing Toughness).
    

### Smite

- **Cost:** 2
    
- **Range:** Smarts
    
- **Duration:** 5
    
- **Effect:** Imbues a weapon or ammunition with energy. The weapon's damage is increased by +1d6. A Raise increases the bonus to +2d6.
