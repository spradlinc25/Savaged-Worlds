# Super Powers List 

## Absorption (2)

- **Effect:** Free Soak roll using _Focus_ vs. damage from a specific Power Type.
    
- **Modifiers:** Achilles Heel (-1), Additional Power Types (+1 to +8), Growth (+3), Mastery (+1), Reflection (+2), Transference (+2), Transmute (+3).
    

## Additional Actions (5/10/15)

- **Effect:** 5 pts: Reduce Multi-Action Penalty (MAP) by 2. 10 pts: Reduce MAP by 4. 15 pts: +1 max action (4 total) and reduce MAP by 4.
    
- **Modifiers:** Concentration (+2), Evaluation (+2), Fast Action (+2), Mental Only (-1), Physical Only (-1).
    

## Ageless (1)

- **Effect:** Character does not grow old.
    
- **Modifiers:** Very Old (+2).
    

## Altered Form (2)

- **Effect:** Body composed of energy or matter. No vital organs (immune to Called Shots). Half damage from falls/collisions. Half Knockback.
    
- **Modifiers:** Fall Proof (+1), Grappler (+1), Reach (+1/level), Requires Activation (-1), Sticky (+2), Viscous (+2), Yield (+1).
    

## Animal Companion (3+Size)

- **Effect:** Loyal Wild Card animal companion. Master and pet can share Bennies.
    
- **Modifiers:** Intelligent (+2), Speech (+2), Summonable (+2), Super Powers (+3/+8), Telepathic Link (+1).
    

## Animal Control (Special)

- **Effect:** SPP cost = Size of animal (min 1). Can control multiple up to total level. Range 12". Action/Focus roll.
    
- **Modifiers:** Requires Touch (-2), Summonable (+4), Telepathic Link (+1/+2).
    

## Aquatic (1/2)

- **Effect:** 1 pt: Hold breath 15 mins. 2 pts: Cannot drown, full Pace when swimming.
    
- **Modifiers:** Fast Swimmer (+1/+2).
    

## Armor (1/Level)

- **Effect:** +2 Armor per level. Stacks with natural armor/Toughness power. Combine Armor+Toughness powers cannot exceed Power Limit.
    
- **Modifiers:** Heavy Armor (+4), Partial Protection (-1/-2), Requires Activation (-1), Sealed (+2).
    

## Awareness (1/Level)

- **Effect:** Ignore 1 point of attack penalties (Dodge, speed, etc.) per level. No effect on Parry/Block.
    
- **Modifiers:** Requires Activation (-1).
    

## Blind (2)

- **Effect:** Range 12". Focus vs Vigor. Success: -2 to actions requiring sight. Raise: -4. Recovery: free Vigor roll at end of turn.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+3), Strong (+1).
    

## Boost/Lower Trait (2)

- **Effect:** Range 12". Action/Focus roll. Increase/decrease Trait 1 die type (2 with raise). Lasts until end of target's next turn. Max +/- 2 steps.
    
- **Modifiers:** Additional Recipients (+1), Any Trait (+2), Leech (+2), Power (+2), Requires Touch (-2).
    

## Broadcast (2)

- **Effect:** See/hear radio/TV/Internet signals within 10 miles. Action. Encrypted signals require Focus -2.
    
- **Modifiers:** Channel Surfer (+1), Manipulation (+1/+3), Range (+1/+2).
    

## Burrowing (1)

- **Effect:** Move half Pace through soft earth. Surprise attack vs Notice: Success = Vulnerable, Raise = Drop.
    
- **Modifiers:** Block Buster (+1), Pace (+1), Tunneler (+1).
    

## Chameleon (3)

- **Effect:** Assume appearance of creature within 2 Size levels. Action/Focus roll.
    
- **Modifiers:** Inanimate Object (+2), Biometrics (+2), Requires Touch (-2).
    

## Construct (8)

- **Effect:** +2 to recover from Shaken. Ignore 1 point of Wound penalties. No breath/eat. Immune to poison/disease. No Bleeding Out. Heal with Repair (1 hr/wound).
    
- **Modifiers:** Dependency (-2).
    

## Copycat (1/Level)

- **Effect:** Mimic powers of target within 6". Action/Focus roll (opposed by Spirit). Max SPP = level. Duration: 1 hour. No Devices/Magic by default.
    
- **Modifiers:** Arcane (+2), Devices (+2), Duration (+3), Overly Accurate (-2), Partial Power (+2), Requires Touch (-2).
    

## Damage Field (Special)

- **Effect:** Aura deals damage to adjacent targets at end of user's turn. Evasion roll to negate. 2d6 (3 SPP), 3d6 (5 SPP), 4d6 (10 SPP).
    
- **Modifiers:** AP (+1 per 2 AP), Area Effect (+2/+4), Lethal (-1), Permanent (-2).
    

## Deadeye (2)

- **Effect:** +d6 damage with mundane firearms/bows. Does not stack with Ranged Attack power.
    
- **Modifiers:** Deadly (+1: increases to d10).
    

## Decay (2)

- **Effect:** Focus vs Vigor (at -2 with raise) within 6". Success: 1 Wound. Destroys objects Hardness 12 or less.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Midas Touch (-2), Strong (+2), Requires Touch (-2).
    

## Dodge (1-5)

- **Effect:** Subtract 1 per level from Shooting/Athletics (throwing) attacks. Max -5. Stacks with others to max -10.
    
- **Modifiers:** Defender (+2/+4), Deflect (+2), Requires Activation (-1).
    

## Doesn't Breathe (1)

- **Effect:** Hold breath 15 mins.
    
- **Modifiers:** Extreme (+1: Never breathe, immune to vacuum/inhaled toxins).
    

## Doesn't Eat (1)

- **Effect:** Go twice as long without food.
    
- **Modifiers:** Extreme (+1: Never eat/drink).
    

## Doesn't Sleep (1)

- **Effect:** Need half normal sleep.
    
- **Modifiers:** Tireless (+1: Never sleep).
    

## Duplication (4/Level)

- **Effect:** Create 1 Extra clone per level. Focus roll. Clone has all powers except Duplication. Clones vanish if parent is Incapacitated.
    
- **Modifiers:** Equipped (+2), Leashed (-2), No Tell (+1), Promotion (+2).
    

## Earthquake (2)

- **Effect:** Template (Cone, LBT, or 2"x12" line). Action. Targets must Evade or be Shaken. Critical Failure = 3d6 damage.
    
- **Modifiers:** Strong (+2).
    

## Energy Control (5)

- **Effect:** Manipulate one Energy Power Type in MBT sphere within 12". Move 12"/turn. Effects: Damage (2d6), Dampen (+4 resist), Darken (Dim), Distract.
    
- **Modifiers:** Add Type (+1), Area Effect (+2), Power (+5: Damage 4d6, Immunity, Dark, Distracted+Vulnerable), Requires Material (-1/-2).
    

## Entangle (3)

- **Effect:** Range 12". Focus vs Athletics. Success: Entangled. Raise: Bound. Hardness 8.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Deadly (+2/+4), Requires Material (-1/-2), Requires Touch (-2), Strong (+2).
    

## Environmental Resistance (1)

- **Effect:** Resistant to one Power Type. Reduce damage by 4. +4 to resist effects.
    
- **Modifiers:** Add Type (+1), Area Effect (+2/+4), Immunity (+2 per Type), Requires Activation (-1).
    

## Explode (2)

- **Effect:** LBT centered on hero. Focus roll. Evade (-2 with raise) or 3d10 damage (4d10 with raise). Hero is Shaken after.
    
- **Modifiers:** Big Bang (+2: 5d10, but Stunned), Failsafe (+1), Temporary Disintegration (-1).
    

## Extra Limbs (2/Level)

- **Effect:** Each level adds a pair/limb. +1 Gang Up bonus per level.
    
- **Modifiers:** Reach (+1/level).
    

## Fear (2)

- **Effect:** Target within 6". Focus roll. Victim makes Fear test (-2 with raise). Target only once per encounter.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Requires Touch (-2), Strong (+2).
    

## Fearless (2)

- **Effect:** Immune to Fear checks and Intimidation.
    
- **Modifiers:** Steady (+2: allies within Command Range get +2 on Fear checks).
    

## Flight (2-18)

- **Effect:** Pace 6 (2), 12 (4), 24 (5), 45 (6), 90 (8), 180 (10), 360 (12), Sonic (14), Super Sonic (16), Near Light (18). High speeds grant attack penalties to foes.
    
- **Modifiers:** FTL (+1), Glider (-1), Maneuverable (+1), Ungainly (-2).
    

## Force Field (1/Level)

- **Effect:** Reduce incoming damage by 1 per level (max 10).
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Life Support (+2), Mobile (+1), Requires Activation (-1).
    

## Genius (2)

- **Effect:** Free reroll on all Smarts and Smarts-based skills.
    
- **Modifiers:** Fast Learner (+1).
    

## Growth (3/Level)

- **Effect:** Each level: +1 Size, +1 Toughness, +1 Strength die step. Large (Pace +2, Reach +1), Huge (Pace +4, Reach +2), Gargantuan (Pace +6, Reach +3).
    
- **Modifiers:** Permanent (-2), Swat (+1/+2).
    

## Hardy (2)

- **Effect:** Second Shaken result does not cause a Wound.
    

## Healing (3)

- **Effect:** Range 6". Focus roll. Heals 1 Wound (2 with raise). One attempt per "set" of Wounds. Within "Golden Hour".
    
- **Modifiers:** Cure (+2), Fatigue (+2), Refresh (+2), Requires Touch (-2), Restoration (+2), Resurrection (+2).
    

## Heightened Senses (Special)

- **Effect:** 1 pt per sense. Eagle Eyes (Range -1), Hearing (+2 Notice), Infravision, Low Light Vision, Microscopic Vision, Smell (+2 Survival), X-Ray Vision.
    

## Illusion (4)

- **Effect:** MBT within 12". Action/Focus. Foes make Smarts -2 to disbelieve.
    
- **Modifiers:** After Effects (+1), Area Effect (+2), Distraction (+1), Film Quality (+1), Obscurement (+2), System Shock (+2).
    

## Immune to Poison/Disease (1)

- **Effect:** Immune to one. (2 SPP for both).
    
- **Modifiers:** Cure Disease (+2), Cure Poison (+1).
    

## Infection (2)

- **Effect:** Range 6". Focus vs Vigor (-2 with raise). Success: Fatigue. Repeats every turn until success (raise gives immunity).
    
- **Modifiers:** Affliction (+2), Area Effect (+2/+4), Contagious (+1), Duration (+1), Lethal (+1), Mutation (+1), Replication (+1), Requires Touch (-2), Strong (+2).
    

## Intangibility (5)

- **Effect:** Action to activate. Walk through walls. Immune to physical attacks (Energy affects normally). Opposed Focus vs Vigor/Athletics to deal 1 Wound.
    
- **Modifiers:** Affect Others (+2), Permanent (-2), Requires Activation (-1).
    

## Interface (2)

- **Effect:** +2 Electronics/Hacking.
    
- **Modifiers:** Code Breaker (+1), Fast (+2), Range (+1).
    

## Invisibility (8)

- **Effect:** Action to activate. -4 to Notice or hit the character. Gear included.
    
- **Modifiers:** Mobile (+1), Permanent (-2), Personal (-2), Projection (+3/+6), True Invisibility (+2: -6 penalty).
    

## Jinx (4)

- **Effect:** Foe fails roll vs hero: becomes Vulnerable. Critical Failure: Shaken too.
    
- **Modifiers:** Greater Jinx (+2: Crit Fail = Stunned).
    

## Leaping (1-8)

- **Effect:** 1 pt: 2"V/4"H. 2 pts: 4"V/8"H. 3 pts: 8"V/16"H. 4 pts: 16"V/32"H. 5 pts: 32"V/64"H. 8 pts: Half mile V/One mile H.
    
- **Modifiers:** Bounce (+1), Death From Above (+1).
    

## Machine Control (Special)

- **Effect:** SPP cost = Size of machine. Mentally operate electronic machines within 12". Focus roll (at -2 or more).
    
- **Modifiers:** Multi-Task (+2/+4), Requires Touch (-2).
    

## Malfunction (3)

- **Effect:** Range 12". Focus roll vs Penalty (Simple 0, Moderate -2, Complex -4). Device stops working. Raise: user is Distracted.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Requires Touch (-2).
    

## Matter Control (5)

- **Effect:** Manipulate one Matter Power Type in MBT sphere within 12". Effects: Barrier (Hardness 10), Damage (2d6), Dampen, Distract.
    
- **Modifiers:** Add Type (+1), Area Effect (+2), Power (+5), Requires Material (-1/-2).
    

## Melee Attack (Special)

- **Effect:** Str+d6 (2), Str+2d6 (4), Str+3d6 (6). Stacks with Edges/Weapons.
    
- **Specific Attack Forms:** Bite (+1: Str+d4), Claws (+2: Str+d4, +2 Climb), Horns (+1: Str+d4, +4 on 5" charge), Special Weapon (+2: +d6 damage, max +5d6 total).
    
- **Modifiers:** AP (+1 per 2 AP), Charge (+1), Smash (+3), Thrown (+2).
    

## Mind Control (5)

- **Effect:** Range 12". Focus vs Spirit (-2 with raise). Target obeys until released or breaks free. Ordering self-harm allows Smarts roll to resist.
    
- **Modifiers:** Forgetful (+2), Leashed (-2), Multiple Minds (+2), Requires Touch (-2), Smarts (+1), Strong (+2).
    

## Mind Reading (3)

- **Effect:** Range 6". Focus vs Smarts (-2 with raise). Detect surface thoughts. "Deep Dive" (10 mins) for unconscious targets.
    
- **Modifiers:** Memory Mastery (+3/+5), Requires Touch (-2), Strong (+2).
    

## Mind Shield (1)

- **Effect:** -2 to be affected by Mind Reading/Control. Successful resist Fatigues the attacker.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Strong (+2).

# Super Powers List (N-Z)

## Minions (1 Per Minion)

- **Effect:** Each level provides one loyal Extra. Base stats: Agility/Strength/Vigor d6, Smarts d4, Spirit d6. Skills at d6.
    
- **Modifiers:** Resilient (+1), Summonable (+2), Super Powers (+3/+5).
    

## Negation (3)

- **Effect:** Range 6". Focus vs Spirit (-2 with raise). Negate one of target's powers. Free Spirit roll at start of turns to recover.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Full Spectrum (+3), Requires Touch (-2), Strong (+2).
    

## No Vital Organs (1)

- **Effect:** Called Shots do no additional damage.
    

## Object Reading (2)

- **Effect:** Range 6". Focus roll to detect history/use of an item.
    
- **Modifiers:** Requires Touch (-2).
    

## Obscure (4)

- **Effect:** LBT sphere within 12". Creates Dim or Dark Illumination. Move 12"/turn.
    
- **Modifiers:** Additional Sense (+1), Area Effect (+2), Self (-1), Strong (+2: Pitch Darkness).
    

## Pace (2)

- **Effect:** Base Pace +2, Running Die +1 step. Stacks with Fleet-Footed.
    
- **Modifiers:** Strider (+1: ignore Difficult Ground).
    

## Parry (1-5)

- **Effect:** +1 Parry per level (max +5).
    
- **Modifiers:** Deflect (+2), Protector (+2: grant bonus to adjacent allies).
    

## Poison (2)

- **Effect:** Range 6". Focus vs Vigor (-2 with raise). Success: Fatigue. Repeats until success (raise = immunity).
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Deadly (+1), Strong (+2), Requires Touch (-2).
    

## Possession (5)

- **Effect:** Range 6". Focus vs Spirit (-2 with raise). Take over target's body; user's body collapses.
    
- **Modifiers:** Forgetful (+2), Memories (+2), Requires Touch (-2), Strong (+2).
    

## Push (1)

- **Effect:** Cone within Smarts range. Focus roll. Everyone inside is Distracted and must roll Strength or be knocked back 1d6".
    
- **Modifiers:** Strong (+2: Strength roll at -2, 2d6" knockback).
    

## Ranged Attack (Special)

- **Effect:** Damage 2d6 (3), 3d6 (6), 4d6 (9), 5d6 (12), 6d6 (15). Range 12/24/48.
    
- **Modifiers:** AP (+1/2AP), Area Effect (+2/+4), Charge (+1), Cone (+0/+1), Lethal (-1), RoF (+3/+6), Requires Material (-2), Spread (+1).
    

## Reach (1-3)

- **Effect:** Natural limbs have Reach +1 to +3.
    

## Regeneration (2/5/10)

- **Effect:** Natural healing roll to recover Wounds. 2 pts: Daily. 5 pts: Hourly. 10 pts: Every round.
    
- **Modifiers:** Destruction (-1), Relief (+2/+3), Regrowth (+2).
    

## Scan (2)

- **Effect:** Detect source of specific Power Type within 1 mile. Action.
    
- **Modifiers:** Add Type (+1), Calibration (+2), Distance (+1/+2), Tracker (+1).
    

## Shape Change (2/Level)

- **Effect:** Transform into natural animals. Size range is +/- level. Action/Focus roll.
    
- **Modifiers:** Powers (+4), Primal (-4), Retention (+4), Speech (+1).
    

## Shrink (4)

- **Effect:** Reduce Size up to 2 steps. -1 Str roll/Size step.
    
- **Modifiers:** Density (+5), Microscopic (+3), Quick Change (+1), Smaller (+8/+16).
    

## Skill Bonus (2/4)

- **Effect:** +1 (2 pts) or +2 (4 pts) to a single skill. Total cost across all skills capped by Power Limit.
    

## Slow (2)

- **Effect:** Range 12". Focus roll vs target. Success: Halve Pace. Raise: Movement becomes an action.
    
- **Modifiers:** Additional Recipients (+1).
    

## Spacer (2)

- **Effect:** Survive space and underwater. Ignore lack of oxygen/pressure.
    
- **Modifiers:** Requires Activation (-1), Shareable (+1).
    

## Speak Language (1)

- **Effect:** Can speak any language. Fluent in hours.
    
- **Modifiers:** Written Word (+1).
    

## Speed (3-17)

- **Effect:** Increased Pace and FOE attack penalties. Pace 12 (3 pts) to Near Light Speed (17 pts). Penalties from 0 to -10.
    
- **Modifiers:** Maneuverable (+1), Pummel (+4), Surface Tension (+1), Vibrate (+5), Ungainly (-2).
    

## Stun (3)

- **Effect:** Range 6". Focus vs Vigor (-2 with raise). Success: Stunned.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2/+4), Requires Touch (-2), Strong (+2).
    

## Super Attribute (2/Level)

- **Effect:** Increase Attribute and its max by 1 die type.
    
- **Modifiers:** Not Today (+2: substitute for different attribute roll once/session).
    

## Super Edge (2/Level)

- **Effect:** Gain a non-Legendary Edge regardless of Rank.
    

## Super Skill (1/Level)

- **Effect:** Increase Skill and its max by 1 die type.
    

## Super Science (4)

- **Effect:** Science d10+ required. Spent Benny on any Power Stunt (any power). Active lasts 2 rounds; passive 5.
    
- **Modifiers:** Overload (-1).
    

## Super Sorcery (4)

- **Effect:** Occult d10+ required. Spent Benny on any Power Stunt (any power). Active lasts 2 rounds; passive 5.
    
- **Modifiers:** Backlash (-1).
    

## Swinging (2)

- **Effect:** Pace 12 movement via cords/webbing. Supports 500 lbs.
    
- **Modifiers:** Strong Line (+1 per 1,000 lbs).
    

## Telekinesis (3)

- **Effect:** Strength d6. Range 12". Move objects 6"/turn.
    
- **Modifiers:** Fine Control (+3), Flight (+2), Power (Variable: 2 pts per +1 Strength die step).
    

## Telepathy (2)

- **Effect:** Communicate with intelligent minds within 24".
    
- **Modifiers:** Range (+2), Mind Rider (+3), Switchboard (+2).
    

## Teleport (2)

- **Effect:** Disappear and reappear up to 12" away. Action.
    
- **Modifiers:** Portal (+2), Range (+1/+2), Rapid Teleport (+1), Redirect (+4), Teleport Other (+5), Traverse (+3).
    

## Toughness (1/Level)

- **Effect:** +1 Toughness. Combined bonus with Armor power cannot exceed Power Limit.
    
- **Modifiers:** Requires Activation (-1).
    

## Uncanny Reflexes (3)

- **Effect:** Ignore -2 Evasion penalty. Gain Evasion vs area attacks that don't usually allow it.
    
- **Modifiers:** Requires Activation (-1).
    

## Undead (8)

- **Effect:** +2 Toughness. +2 Shaken recovery. No breath/eat. Immune to poison/disease. Ignore 1 Wound penalty. No Bleeding Out.
    
- **Modifiers:** Spark of Life (+2).
    

## Vehicle (1)

- **Effect:** Size 0, Top Speed 60 MPH, Toughness 9(2). Replaced if destroyed.
    
- **Modifiers:** Armored (+2/+4), Bulletproof Glass (+1), Enclosed (+1), Flight (+1), Weapons (Special).
    

## Wall Walker (1)

- **Effect:** Walk on walls at normal Pace; inverted at half Pace.
    
- **Modifiers:** Strong Grip (+1).
    

## Weather Control (7)

- **Effect:** Environmental Resistance (Air, Electric, Fire, Water). Manipulate weather within 5 miles.
    
- **Modifiers:** Outdoor Only (-2).
    

## Whirlwind (3)

- **Effect:** MBT within 12". Strength roll or 2d6" knockback. Everyone inside is Distracted.
    
- **Modifiers:** Area Effect (+2), Damage (+2), Cone (+0/+1), Strong (+2).
