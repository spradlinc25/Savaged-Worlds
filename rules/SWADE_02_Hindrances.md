# Hindrances

Hindrances are character flaws, physical limitations, or binding oaths. They provide points during character creation (Minor = 1 point, Major = 2 points). Characters may take up to 4 points of Hindrances.

## Complete Hindrances List

### All Thumbs (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character is mechanically inept. They subtract 2 from skill rolls when using modern or electronic devices, including firing modern weapons.
    

### Anemic (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character is particularly susceptible to sickness and environmental hazards. They subtract 2 from Vigor rolls made to resist Fatigue from hazards (cold, heat, radiation), poison, or disease.
    

### Arrogant (Major)

- **Effect:** The character believes they are the best and must constantly prove it. They must challenge the toughest opponent in any combat and belittle lesser foes.
    

### Bad Eyes (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character has poor vision.
    
    - **Minor:** Subtracts 2 from any Trait roll dependent on vision beyond 5” (10 yards).
        
    - **Major:** The character subtracts 2 from _all_ Trait rolls dependent on vision. (In modern settings, glasses negate the penalty but can be lost or broken).
        

### Bad Luck (Major)

- **Effect:** The character starts each session with one less Benny than usual. (This cannot reduce starting Bennies below zero).
    

### Big Mouth (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character cannot keep a secret and constantly blurts out sensitive information.
    

### Blind (Major)

- **Effect:** The character cannot see. They suffer a -6 penalty to all physical tasks that require vision (Fighting, Shooting, Notice). They subtract 2 from social skill rolls due to eye contact/body language issues. To compensate, they gain a free Edge of their choice (must meet requirements).
    

### Bloodthirsty (Major)

- **Effect:** The character is a killer. They never take prisoners unless forced to. They subtract 4 from Persuasion rolls if their cruel nature is known.
    

### Can’t Swim (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character subtracts 2 from Athletics rolls made to swim. Their Pace in water is 1”.
    

### Cautious (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character over-plans and hesitates. They refuse to take action without a deliberate, heavily researched plan.
    

### Clumsy (Major)

- **Effect:** The character is uncoordinated. They subtract 2 from Athletics and Stealth rolls.
    

### Code of Honor (Major)

- **Effect:** The character keeps their word and adheres to a strict moral code. They will not attack unarmed foes or take advantage of the helpless.
    

### Curious (Major)

- **Effect:** The character cannot resist a mystery. They must investigate anything strange or hidden, often putting the party in danger.
    

### Death Wish (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character wants to die fulfilling some grand, specific cause. They will carelessly throw their life away if it aligns with their goal.
    

### Delusional (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character believes something that is not true.
    
    - **Minor:** The delusion is mostly harmless or an eccentricity.
        
    - **Major:** The delusion causes immediate danger to the character or party (e.g., believing they are immune to bullets).
        

### Doubting Thomas (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character strictly believes in science and denies the supernatural. They suffer a -2 penalty to Fear checks caused by supernatural creatures or events.
    

### Driven (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character’s actions are dictated by a specific goal.
    
    - **Minor:** The goal is a heavy priority but doesn't usually cause harm.
        
    - **Major:** The goal is an obsession; they will risk their life and the lives of allies to achieve it.
        

### Elderly (Major)

- **Effect:** The character is advanced in age. Their Pace is reduced by 1. They subtract 1 from their maximum Agility, Strength, and Vigor (usually to d10). To compensate for their experience, they gain 5 extra skill points which may only be spent on Smarts-based skills.
    

### Enemy (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** Someone is actively trying to thwart or kill the character.
    
    - **Minor:** A lone individual or small, weak group.
        
    - **Major:** A powerful organization, government, or highly lethal individual.
        

### Environmental Weakness (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character is particularly vulnerable to a specific element or hazard (e.g., fire, cold, radiation). They take +4 damage from that source and subtract 4 from rolls made to resist its effects.
    

### Greedy (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is obsessed with acquiring wealth.
    
    - **Minor:** Will argue bitterly over loot shares.
        
    - **Major:** Will kill, betray, or risk their life for money.
        

### Habit (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character has a repetitive behavior or addiction.
    
    - **Minor:** A distracting tic or gross habit. Subtracts 1 from Charisma/Persuasion where relevant.
        
    - **Major:** A physical addiction. If they do not get their fix within 24 hours, they suffer Fatigue every 24 hours until Incapacitated.
        

### Hard of Hearing (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character has poor hearing.
    
    - **Minor:** Subtract 4 from Notice rolls made to hear sounds.
        
    - **Major:** The character is completely deaf. They automatically fail audio-based Notice rolls. They subtract 4 from Notice rolls to detect approaching threats they cannot see.
        

### Heroic (Major)

- **Effect:** The character is a true hero. They must help anyone in need, regardless of the personal cost or danger.
    

### Hesitant (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character freezes under pressure. In combat, they must draw two Action Cards and take the lower of the two. (If they draw a Joker, they may keep it).
    

### Illiterate (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character cannot read or write any language.
    

### Impulsive (Major)

- **Effect:** The character acts before thinking. They will rush into danger, open mysterious doors, and speak without considering consequences.
    

### Jealous (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is deeply insecure.
    
    - **Minor:** Constantly complains or belittles those who have what they want.
        
    - **Major:** Will actively sabotage or betray allies out of envy.
        

### Loyal (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character will never leave a friend behind or betray their allies, even if it means sacrificing their own life.
    

### Mean (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character is ill-tempered and disagreeable. They subtract 1 from Persuasion rolls.
    

### Mild Mannered (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character is non-threatening. They subtract 2 from Intimidation rolls.
    

### Mute (Major)

- **Effect:** The character cannot speak. They must communicate through writing or sign language.
    

### Obese (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character is heavily overweight. Their Size increases by +1 (and Toughness by +1). However, their Pace is reduced by 1 and their running die is a d4.
    

### One Arm (Major)

- **Effect:** The character is missing an arm. They subtract 4 from tasks that require two hands (like climbing or using a two-handed weapon).
    

### One Eye (Major)

- **Effect:** The character lacks depth perception. They subtract 2 from any Trait roll that requires depth perception, including Shooting, throwing (Athletics), or related Notice rolls.
    

### Outsider (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character belongs to an unpopular minority or foreign culture.
    
    - **Minor:** They subtract 2 from Persuasion rolls made with the dominant culture.
        
    - **Major:** In addition to the penalty, the character has no legal rights and is actively persecuted or targeted by the authorities.
        

### Overconfident (Major)

- **Effect:** The character believes they can accomplish any task and defeat any foe. They never retreat and always take the most direct, often dangerous, approach.
    

### Pacifist (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character abhors violence.
    
    - **Minor:** Will only fight in self-defense and prefers non-lethal solutions.
        
    - **Major:** Will completely refuse to fight living creatures under any circumstances.
        

### Phobia (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character has an irrational fear of something specific (spiders, heights, darkness).
    
    - **Minor:** Subtract 1 from all Trait rolls in the presence of the phobia.
        
    - **Major:** Subtract 2 from all Trait rolls in the presence of the phobia.
        

### Poverty (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character is chronically broke. They start with half the normal starting funds and find it impossible to hold on to wealth.
    

### Quirk (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character has a minor, memorable behavioral eccentricity (e.g., speaks in the third person, meticulously cleans weapons during conversations).
    

### Ruthless (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character will do whatever it takes to achieve their goals.
    
    - **Minor:** Willing to lie, cheat, or steal from enemies.
        
    - **Major:** Willing to harm innocents or betray allies to get the job done.
        

### Secret (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is hiding something that would ruin them if discovered.
    
    - **Minor:** Would cause embarrassment or minor legal trouble.
        
    - **Major:** Would cause death, long-term imprisonment, or total exile.
        

### Shamed (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is widely known for a terrible disgrace or failure. They subtract 1 (Minor) or 2 (Major) from Persuasion rolls with those who know of the shame.
    

### Slow (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is physically sluggish.
    
    - **Minor:** Pace is reduced by 1, and the running die is a d4.
        
    - **Major:** Pace is reduced by 2, running die is a d4, and they subtract 2 from Athletics rolls and rolls to resist Athletic-based Tests.
        

### Small (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character is very short or slight. Their Size is reduced by 1 (which reduces Toughness by 1).
    

### Stubborn (Minor)

- **Effect:** The character never admits they are wrong and will always double down on their chosen course of action.
    

### Suspicious (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is intensely paranoid.
    
    - **Minor:** Distrusts strangers and questions everyone's motives.
        
    - **Major:** Distrusts even their allies. They subtract 2 from Support rolls made to help allies, and allies subtract 2 from Support rolls made to help them.
        

### Thin Skinned (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character's ego is easily bruised.
    
    - **Minor:** They subtract 2 when resisting Taunt Tests.
        
    - **Major:** In addition to the penalty, if an attacker gets a Raise on a Taunt Test against the character, the character is Shaken instead of just Distracted or Vulnerable.
        

### Tongue-Tied (Major)

- **Effect:** The character stumbles over their words under pressure. They subtract 2 from Intimidation, Persuasion, and Taunt rolls.
    

### Ugly (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is physically unattractive.
    
    - **Minor:** Subtract 1 from Persuasion rolls.
        
    - **Major:** Subtract 2 from Persuasion rolls.
        

### Vengeful (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character cannot let a slight go unpunished.
    
    - **Minor:** Will seek payback, usually non-lethally or socially.
        
    - **Major:** Will seek total destruction or death of those who wronged them.
        

### Vow (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character has sworn a binding oath.
    
    - **Minor:** An oath that dictates behavior but doesn't constantly endanger them.
        
    - **Major:** An all-encompassing oath that governs their life and frequently puts them in extreme danger.
        

### Wanted (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is hunted by the law.
    
    - **Minor:** Wanted for a lesser crime; local authorities are searching for them.
        
    - **Major:** Wanted for a major crime; highly lethal authorities or bounty hunters are actively pursuing them everywhere.
        

### Yellow (Major)

- **Effect:** The character is a coward. They subtract 2 from Fear checks and from rolls made to resist Intimidation.
    

### Young (Minor or Major)

- **Effect:** The character is a child or adolescent.
    
    - **Minor (Adolescent):** They have only 4 Attribute points and 10 Skill points to spend at creation. They start with one extra Benny per session.
        
    - **Major (Child):** They have only 3 Attribute points and 10 Skill points. Their Size is reduced by 1 (reducing Toughness by 1). They start with two extra Bennies per session.
